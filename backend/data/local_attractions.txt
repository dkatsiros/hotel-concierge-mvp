Local Attractions Near Grand Hotel Example

City Art Museum - 0.3 miles
World-class art collection spanning five centuries. Open Tue-Sun 10 AM - 5 PM. Admission $20, free on first Fridays.

Riverside Park - 0.5 miles
Beautiful waterfront park with walking trails, bike rentals, and seasonal outdoor concerts. Open dawn to dusk.

Historic Downtown District - 0.2 miles
Charming streets lined with boutique shops, cafes, and historic architecture. Self-guided walking tour maps available at the concierge desk.

Central Market - 0.4 miles
Indoor market featuring local artisans, fresh produce, and street food vendors. Open daily 8 AM - 6 PM.

City Observation Tower - 0.8 miles
360-degree panoramic views from the 60th floor. Open daily 9 AM - 10 PM. Tickets $25.

Theater District - 0.6 miles
Multiple venues hosting Broadway shows, concerts, and comedy acts. Ask the concierge for current shows and ticket assistance.

Botanical Gardens - 1.2 miles
15 acres of themed gardens and greenhouses. Open daily 9 AM - 5 PM. Admission $15.

Waterfront Dining Row - 0.3 miles
A collection of highly rated restaurants along the river. Reservations recommended for dinner — our concierge can assist.
